Frequently Asked Questions

How do I confirm a reservation?

We assign a dedicated Travel Counselor to process your reservation. Once hotels and itinerary are finalized, a proforma invoice is shared. Upon confirmation, we proceed with the booking.

How much advance do I pay and when?

A 10â€“25% advance is required to confirm reservations. Final payment timelines vary based on hotels, destination, and season.

What is a Confirmation Voucher?

After receiving advance payment, a confirmation voucher is emailed containing all reservation details, which must be shown at check-in.

How do I know if my hotel reservation is confirmed?

The voucher includes confirmation number and hotel contact details. You may verify directly with the hotel if needed.

Do I need to reconfirm with the hotel?

No, we also send a copy of the confirmation voucher directly to the hotel.

How long does confirmation take?

24 to 48 hours.

Cancellation / Modification Policy

Cancellation or modification is subject to hotel terms and seasonality. A 10% processing fee applies for any changes.
